This application connects to proprietary third-party network services that are not under your control.

When you configure a self-hosted Tier 3/4 backend, that server uses yt-dlp to resolve and acquire media from YouTube (and similar platforms) for search, podcast, and extended playback paths.

The client also contacts TheAudioDB for artist photos and biographies in catalog search and artist pages, and the iTunes Search API for chart and album metadata.

Enable Air-Gap Mode in Settings to block outbound requests from the app on this device. Traffic to your Tier 3/4 host (including yt-dlp) is separate and depends on how you run that server.
